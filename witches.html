The Good Witch of the South is Glinda the Good. She is the most powerful of the Witches. According to the Soldier with Green Whiskers, Glinda:

"knows how to keep young in spite of the many years she has lived."" — The Wonderful Wizard of Oz, chapter 18, "Away to the South"

So she was the most beautiful of the four witches. Later books in Baum's Oz series refer to Glinda as a Sorceress, rather than a Witch.

The Wicked Witch of the East is believe to be more powerful than the Good Witch of the North, but she is not as powerful as Glinda, the Good Witch of the South. The Wicked Witch of the East:

"was a middle aged, malevolent woman who conquered and tyraniized the Munchkin Country in Oz's eastern quadrant" — The Wizard of Oz (1900)

The Wicked Witch of The West is the malevolent ruler of the Winkie County. her castle is described as beautiful instead of being a sinister fortress show in the movie. Despite her fear of water, The Wicked Witch of the West:

"was one of the most powerful witches in all of Oz." - The Wizard of Oz (1900)"
